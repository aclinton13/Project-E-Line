Welcome to the Project E-line Webapp

Project E-line was created to help save time during an emergency. After Hurricane Sandy and other natural disasters, a lot of people lost their homes and didn't know the nearest homeless shelter

Our objective is to to provide locations of necessary resources in the event of a natural disaster

We strive to provide you with the best information incase of an emergency


All it takes is to analyze the users locationto identfiy where necessary ressources are nearby and provide importatnt hotline for specific disasters
